🇰🇷 Why does this matter in Korea?

갱년기 · Menopause

😶
Often seen as something to "just endure" → many women don't get proper care or support
💊
Emotional changes are often mistaken as a personality problem, not a health issue
💔
The period when moms hurt their family the most — and get hurt the most in return

중2병 · Jung-i-bbyeong

📚
Not just showing off — it's an explosion of academic pressure + identity crisis
🥊
Peer conflicts increase at this age → but society often dismisses it as "just puberty"
🏠
Two emotionally volatile people clash daily → family communication breaks down
